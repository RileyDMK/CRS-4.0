Tuition

I charge a monthly tuition based on a per-lesson fee. For Artist Preparatory students I charge $45.00 for a 75 minute lesson. For Recreational Piano students I charge $35.00 for a 45 minute lesson. Shorter lessons and reduced rates, if appropriate, are handled on a case-by-case basis.

Your first lesson is free so that we may determine if the student-to-teacher match is appropriate to continue.
