“Make the instrument sing!” has been the advice of every great teacher. But how about actual singing?

The human voice is the most versatile and convenient instrument you will ever master. When one learns to sing with confidence life gets a little easier and a lot more fun.

Regardless of your current level or ability, I can teach you to improve your singing, whether solo, as part of a choral group or even a jazz choir.

In college I studied voice and sang with a variety of classical, jazz, and acapella choirs. Over the past ten years I’ve been the featured vocalist with a dance band in Spokane. I teach students how to breathe as a singer, how to stay on pitch, control their volume, and how to “sell” their music to an appreciative audience.
