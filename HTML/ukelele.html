Ukulele Lessons

The ukulele is a get-happy and versatile instrument that is enjoying a well-deserved renaissance. With only four strings, learning to play the ukulele is easier than the guitar, but learning it first can help you make the transition to guitar much easier if that’s your goal.

Ukes come in four sizes and ranges: Tenor, Baritone, Concert and Soprano. If you haven’t bought your uke yet I can help you with that decision.

I’ll have you playing popular and classic songs that you particularly love. I’ll help you determine whether the song is “in your range” in case you want to sing along and, if it’s not in your range , how to change the key.
