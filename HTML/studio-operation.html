Studio Operation

Music teaching is my primary business. When you enroll in my studio I will be reserving time for you on a regular basis. I expect every student to come to their lesson having practiced the assigned materials and in a state of mind, and health, to accept direction. If practice has been limited I still want the student to come for a lesson. I strive for proactive communications when needed for cancellations and other correspondence. I expect that assigned music and materials are brought with the student to each lesson and taken home at the end of each lesson.

You can expect a welcoming and comfortable studio, and appreciation for the effort you make to travel to Country Road Studio. You can expect a teacher who is prepared for your lesson, focused entirely on what you need to enhance your musical education. You can expect suitable materials to be explored and assigned as your needs and talents evolve.
