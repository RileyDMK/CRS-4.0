About Bryan Stuart

I teach with the underlying goal that one day my students will no longer need a teacher but will have been taught the skills to tackle new music knowing how to approach it with confidence, practice it effectively, and perform it well enough to experience the joy that comes from that accomplishment.

After ten years of teaching at Music City in Spokane, and having moved to a new home and barn near Rathdrum, ID, it made sense to open a new music studio here and offer lessons to my new neighbors. Country Road Studio is situated within my new barn. You’ll find it convenient to Hwy 95, just down a country road. Click the Contact button for more on my location. Now that I have moved my home to North Idaho, it made sense to open a studio here on our tree farm, just down a country road.




Bryan Stuart received his Bachelor’s degree in Music at the University of North Texas, and his Master’s of Music with emphasis on education, piano and singing at Southern Methodist University. While pursuing a career in institutional advancement, he performed in stage bands and choral groups in Texas, Colorado, Oregon and Washington until he took up music education full time in 2009. Currently, Bryan teaches at Music City and Country Road Studio.
