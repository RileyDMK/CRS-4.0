Lesson Tracks

Once we arrive at an understanding of each student’s goal, with parental input, I recommend that student to one of two tracks for learning: Recreational or Artist Preparatory.

Read More


Recreational

This track is for those whose primary goal is to take it at their own pace, for those who may have taken music lessons years ago and want to have another go at it, and for those who just want to see if they like it enough to get serious. It’s also for parents/guardians hoping their child will “take to” music lessons.



Artist Preparatory

This track is for students who are serious about learning, practicing, and performing, who envision themselves advancing beyond the beginner stage. It’s for those who want to learn to be musicians of depth, and who are willing to organize their time for effective practice and preparation for each lesson.
